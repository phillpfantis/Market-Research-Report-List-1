<p><strong>The Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Controllable Pitch Propeller</li><li>Fixed Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propeller market primarily consists of two main types: controllable pitch propellers (CPP) and fixed pitch propellers (FPP). Controllable pitch propellers allow for adjustable blade angles, optimizing performance for varying operational conditions, enhancing fuel efficiency and maneuverability. In contrast, fixed pitch propellers have a set blade angle, offering simplified design and reduced maintenance but less adaptability. Both types cater to different segments, including commercial shipping, recreational boating, and marine industries, each with specific performance and operational requirements.</p></p>
