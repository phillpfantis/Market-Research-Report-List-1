<p><ul><li>Non-vacuum Stainless Steel Insulated Water Bottle</li><li>Vacuum Stainless Steel Insulated Water Bottle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulated water bottle market can be categorized into two main types: non-vacuum stainless steel insulated water bottles and vacuum stainless steel insulated water bottles. Non-vacuum bottles, while durable and lightweight, offer moderate insulation, making them suitable for short-term use. Conversely, vacuum stainless steel insulated bottles feature a double-wall construction that effectively prevents heat transfer, providing superior temperature retention for hot or cold beverages over extended periods, making them ideal for outdoor activities and long journeys.</p></p>
