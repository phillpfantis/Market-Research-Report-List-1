<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Breathing Apparatus Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Circuit</li><li>Closed-Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Breathing Apparatus market comprises two main types: open-circuit and closed-circuit systems. Open-circuit systems exhale air into the environment, typically found in devices like SCBA (Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus), where fresh air is supplied from a tank. In contrast, closed-circuit systems recycle exhaled air by removing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen, making them more suitable for extended use in confined spaces. Each type caters to specific applications, emphasizing safety and efficiency in various environments, such as firefighting and industrial operations.</p></p>
