<p><p>The automobile rearview mirror market consists of two primary types: side rearview mirrors and windshield rearview mirrors. Side rearview mirrors are mounted on both sides of the vehicle, providing essential visibility of adjacent lanes and reducing blind spots. In contrast, windshield rearview mirrors are centrally positioned on the windshield, offering a wide view of the rear traffic and aiding in navigation. Both types enhance safety and driving comfort but serve distinct functions in improving overall vehicle visibility.</p></p>
