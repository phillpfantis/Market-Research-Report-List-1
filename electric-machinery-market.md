<p><ul><li>Direct Current Machine</li><li>Alternating Current Dynamo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric machinery market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on Direct Current (DC) machines and Alternating Current (AC) dynamos. DC machines are characterized by their ability to provide constant voltage and are widely used in applications requiring reliable and precise control, like electric vehicles. In contrast, AC dynamos convert mechanical energy into electrical energy using alternating currents, suitable for powering larger industrial systems and grid applications. Both types play crucial roles in energy generation, distribution, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
