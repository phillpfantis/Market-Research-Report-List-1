<p><strong>The Intensifying Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rare Earth Intensifying Screen</li><li>Calcium Tungstate Intensifying Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intensifying screen market is primarily divided into Rare Earth Intensifying Screens and Calcium Tungstate Intensifying Screens. Rare Earth Intensifying Screens are known for their higher efficiency and improved image quality, utilizing materials like terbium and lanthanum to enhance radiographic performance. In contrast, Calcium Tungstate Intensifying Screens, traditionally used in radiology, rely on calcium tungstate crystals to convert X-rays into visible light but are less efficient than their rare earth counterparts. Each type serves distinct applications in medical imaging.</p></p>
