<p><ul><li>Analog Output</li><li>Digital Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall Effect Position Sensor Market is divided into two main types based on output: Analog and Digital. Analog output sensors provide a continuous voltage proportional to the magnetic field strength, allowing for precise position measurement and smooth signaling. In contrast, digital output sensors deliver discrete signals, often in binary form, which simplifies integration with digital systems but may offer less nuanced position data. Both types are widely utilized in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics applications for accurate positioning and motion sensing.</p></p>
