<p><strong>The Flash Point Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cup Flash Point Tester</li><li>Closed Cup Flash Point Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flash Point Tester market encompasses two main types: Open Cup Flash Point Testers and Closed Cup Flash Point Testers. Open Cup testers measure the flash point by exposing a sample in an open container to a heat source, making them suitable for high flash point materials. In contrast, Closed Cup testers enclose the sample, allowing for more accurate measurements of low flash point materials under controlled conditions. Both types are essential for assessing flammability and ensuring safety in various industries.</p></p>
