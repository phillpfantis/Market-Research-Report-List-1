<p><ul><li>Wire Bonder</li><li>Die Bonder</li><li>FC Bonder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bonder Market encompasses various types of bonding technologies used in semiconductor manufacturing. Wire Bonders are utilized for connecting semiconductor chips to packages using fine wires, ensuring efficient electrical connections. Die Bonders focus on attaching semiconductor die to substrates or packages with high precision, crucial for reliability. Flip Chip (FC) Bonders apply a process where chips are flipped and directly bonded to the substrate, enhancing performance and reducing package size. Together, these technologies play a vital role in advancing electronics assembly.</p></p>
