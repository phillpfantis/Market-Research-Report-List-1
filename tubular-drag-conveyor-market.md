<p><p>A Tubular Drag Conveyor is a mechanical device used to transport bulk materials in a closed tube, primarily designed for industries like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and mining. This type of conveyor offers advantages such as reduced dust emissions, energy efficiency, and ease of maintenance.</p><p>The Tubular Drag Conveyor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for efficient and hygienic material handling solutions across various sectors.
