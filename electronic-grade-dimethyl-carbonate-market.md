<p><p>The Electronic Grade Dimethyl Carbonate market is primarily categorized by its production methods: the Propylene Oxide Method and the Ethylene Oxide Method. The Propylene Oxide Method involves reacting propylene oxide with carbon dioxide, resulting in a high-purity product suitable for electronics applications. In contrast, the Ethylene Oxide Method utilizes ethylene oxide, leading to a different purity and characteristics. Both methods cater to the growing demand for high-quality solvents and additives in the electronics sector, emphasizing purity and performance.</p></p>
