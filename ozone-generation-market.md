<p><p>The ozone generation market encompasses various technologies, including Corona Discharge, Cold Plasma, Electrolysis, and Ultraviolet (UV) methods. Corona Discharge utilizes high voltage to create ozone from oxygen, making it efficient for industrial applications. Cold Plasma technology generates ozone at lower temperatures, suitable for sensitive environments. Electrolysis produces ozone through an electric current in water, ideal for smaller systems. UV methods use ultraviolet light to convert oxygen into ozone, often employed in water treatment and air purification for its effectiveness and simplicity.</p></p>
