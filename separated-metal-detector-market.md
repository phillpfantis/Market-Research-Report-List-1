<p><p>The separated metal detector market includes three main types: electromagnetic induction, X-ray, and microwave detectors. Electromagnetic induction utilizes magnetic fields to detect metallic objects, making it effective for a variety of applications, including security and industrial uses. X-ray detectors provide detailed imaging to identify metals within objects or packages, enhancing security during inspections. Microwave detectors leverage high-frequency signals to identify metal presence, often used in safety and quality control scenarios. Together, these technologies cater to diverse detection needs across multiple industries.</p></p>
