<p><p>The Automatic Sampling System market encompasses three main types: liquid samplers, gas samplers, and ambient air samplers. Liquid samplers are designed to collect and analyze liquid samples from various sources, such as industrial processes or wastewater. Gas samplers capture gaseous emissions or vapors for environmental monitoring or quality control. Ambient air samplers focus on collecting air samples to assess pollution levels and maintain air quality standards. Each type serves critical roles in environmental monitoring and industrial safety regulations.</p></p>
