<p><p>Wire thread inserts are used to create durable, wear-resistant internal threads in various materials, enhancing the strength of connections. The market primarily covers two types: free-running inserts, which allow for smooth installation and easy adjustments, and screw-locking inserts, designed to prevent loosening from vibration or torque. Free-running inserts are ideal for lightweight applications, while screw-locking inserts offer enhanced stability in dynamic environments. This distinction is crucial in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machinery where thread integrity is essential.</p></p>
