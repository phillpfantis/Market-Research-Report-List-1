<p><strong>The Targeted RNA Sequencing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exome Sequencing</li><li>Enrichment Sequencing</li><li>Amplicon Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The targeted RNA sequencing market is categorized into three main types: exome sequencing, enrichment sequencing, and amplicon sequencing. Exome sequencing focuses on the protein-coding regions of the genome, capturing essential genomic data. Enrichment sequencing selectively isolates specific RNA targets to enhance the detection of low-abundance transcripts. Amplicon sequencing involves the amplification of specific gene regions to analyze variations in gene expression. Each type serves unique research and clinical purposes, facilitating advancements in genomics and precision medicine.</p></p>
