<p><p>The frameless fans market comprises two main types: DC frameless fans and AC frameless fans. DC frameless fans operate on direct current, offering higher energy efficiency, quieter operation, and greater speed control, making them ideal for modern electronic devices and applications. In contrast, AC frameless fans use alternating current, typically providing reliable performance and cost-effectiveness, often suited for industrial and commercial environments. Both types are valued for their compact design and versatility in various cooling and ventilation applications.</p></p>
