<p><strong>The Single-phase Power Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Power Controller</li><li>Nonresistive Power Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase power controller market is divided into two main types: resistive and non-resistive power controllers. Resistive power controllers manage power by adjusting the electrical resistance, primarily used for incandescent lamps and heaters. In contrast, non-resistive power controllers utilize advanced techniques like phase control and zero-crossing methods to regulate power to inductive loads such as motors and transformers. Both types cater to different applications, enhancing energy efficiency and performance in residential and industrial settings.</p></p>
