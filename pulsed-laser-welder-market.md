<p><strong>The Pulsed Laser Welder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Laser</li><li>YAG Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulsed laser welder market primarily includes two types: Fiber Laser and YAG Laser. Fiber lasers utilize a flexible fiber optic to generate high-intensity light, offering precision and efficiency for thin materials and complex geometries. They are favored for their energy efficiency and minimal heat-affected zones. YAG lasers, on the other hand, utilize a solid-state medium, providing robust welding capabilities for thicker materials. They are known for their versatility and effectiveness in various industrial applications, especially in metal fabrication.</p></p>
