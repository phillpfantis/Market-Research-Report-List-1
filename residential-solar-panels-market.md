<p><p>The residential solar panels market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Monocrystalline Solar Panels**: Highly efficient and space-saving, these panels are made from single-crystal silicon, offering higher energy output.</p><p>2. **Polycrystalline Solar Panels**: More affordable, these panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, providing moderate efficiency but requiring more space.</p><p>3. **Thin-Film Amorphous Solar Panels**: Lightweight and flexible, these panels have lower efficiency but can be installed on various surfaces.</p><p>4. **Other Market**: This includes specialized or emerging solar technologies, like BIPV (Building Integrated Photovoltaics) and concentrated solar power systems, catering to niche applications.</p></p>
