<p><p>In the pharmaceutics market, blockchain technology can be categorized into three types: private, public, and consortium blockchains. Private blockchains are controlled by a single organization, ensuring confidentiality and speed, ideal for internal supply chain processes. Public blockchains allow anyone to participate, promoting transparency and trust but may face scalability issues. Consortium blockchains involve a group of organizations collaborating, balancing the benefits of decentralization with governance control, making them suitable for partnerships in drug development and distribution.</p></p>
