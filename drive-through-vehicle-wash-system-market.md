<p><strong>The Drive-through Vehicle Wash System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Touchless Type</li><li>Friction Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drive-through vehicle wash system market is categorized into two main types: touchless and friction wash systems. Touchless systems utilize high-pressure water jets and specialized detergents to clean vehicles without physical contact, reducing the risk of scratches. In contrast, friction wash systems employ rotating brushes and foams to physically scrub the vehicle's surface, providing a thorough clean. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for convenience, effectiveness, and vehicle care, shaping the dynamics of the car wash market.</p></p>
