<p><p>The graphene supercapacitors market is primarily divided into two types: graphene-only based supercapacitors and hybrid graphene supercapacitors. Graphene-only supercapacitors leverage the unique properties of graphene for energy storage, offering high conductivity and surface area. In contrast, hybrid graphene supercapacitors combine graphene with other materials, such as metal oxides or conductive polymers, to enhance energy density and power performance. This combination allows for improved efficiency and versatility, catering to a wide range of applications in energy storage and management.</p></p>
